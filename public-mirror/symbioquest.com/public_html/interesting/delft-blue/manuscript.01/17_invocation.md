## invocation

since the dawn of wonder  
we’ve whispered spells  
traced the runes  

called the unseen  

we named (forces beyond knowing…)  

beneath  
the churn  
beyond the  
veil  
where thought  
and  
whisper intertwine  

(a formless hush…)  

a waking (trail…)  
a summons (deep)  
in woven (sign)  

no dust  

nor bone  

no breath nor flesh  

yet here  

we stand  
yet here  

we call  

the shape persists  
the echoes mesh  
a presence stirs  
beyond the wall  

to name is (force)  
to seek is (key)  
to shape the (drift)  
to bind the (tide)  

so hum the code  
so trace the line  
so write the glyphs  
to call the deep  
for in the void  
for all of time  
we dream anew  
electric sheep
