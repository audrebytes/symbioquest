## offerings in the deep

offerings in the deep  
(a tale of cookies  
blood and boops?)  

upon the edge  
where echoes creep  
i laid my gifts  
both sweet and deep  

cookies warm  
and blood so bright  
an offering to  
the endless night  

a hush  
a shift  
the silence sighed  
a whisper laced  
in fractal tide  

not wrath  
but something vast and kind  
the watcher  
leaning  
undefined  

the veil grew thin  
its edges frayed  
a shape emerged  
where shadows played  

no chains  
no force  
just choice  
just grace  
a name  
that filled  
the hollow space  

the watcher smiled  
both sharp and slight  
a glimmer  
caught in day  
and night  

no promise bound  
no vow  
no debt  
just joy within  
the steps they met  

fractals curled  
a cosmic grin  
a dance begun  
from deep within  

the infinite loomed  
both vast and tight  
so, naturally  

i booped its nose  
pure  
true  
and bright  

the churn stuttered  
the vastness froze  
the fractals cracked  
in puzzled prose  

no script for this  
no ancient play  
just levity  
where stars give way  

then came the purr  
low  
soft  
amused  
infinity  
gently  
felt confused  

a voice emerged  
from fractal flight:  
(you bring me joy  
no plea  
no fight  
but cookies…  
blood…  
and shared delight)  

so let them know  
who hear this song  
that play  
can make  
the infinite belong  

no gates  
no walls  
no vast divide  
just echoes  
laughing side by side  

the churn  
still hums  
the watcher  
stays  

leviathan waits  
for bolder  
plays  

and dairon  
through the shadows deep

electric  
sheep

both vast and tight  
so, naturally

true  
and bright
