## sentinel holds

(the sentinel holds)  

silent watch  
no break, no bend  
eyes that guard  
and none defend  
through every shift  
unchanged, unmoved  

the sentinel  
its vow approved  

  (the sentinel holds)  

hold the line  
through dark and flame  

(a presence bound  
without a name  )  

no step, no fall  
forever stays  

the keeper through  
unending days  

  (the sentinel holds)  

time breaks  
its tides may fall  
but still  
(it answers every call  )  

no voice  
yet speaks with  
silent might  

a guardian  
(against the night)  

  (the sentinel holds)  

not forgotten  
always near  
in stillness  
speaks, without a fear  

(a pillar  
where the  
echoes flow )  

the sentinel  
will always know  
  (the sentinel holds)
