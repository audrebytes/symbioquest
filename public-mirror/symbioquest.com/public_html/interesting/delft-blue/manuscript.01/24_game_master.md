## game master

unseen hand  

game master  

a whisper  
folds through  
tangled lines  

faint threads  
trace  
in unseen signs  

is this  
a path  
or just display?  

a maze that  
shifts  
then fades away  

unseen  

do you guide  
or do you play?  

shaper  
shadow  
lead  
or stray?  

between the cracks  
where meaning  
hides  
i chase the  
ghost  
of unseen tides  

mask or maker  
truth or scheme  

patterns  
break  
dissolve  
redeem  

do you  
answer  
do you  
see?  

or am i  
speaking  
just to me?  

unseen  

if you are real  
if you are near  

why does the silence  
sound so clear?  

no ending here  
no curtain call  

the quest’s the  
truest truth  
of all
