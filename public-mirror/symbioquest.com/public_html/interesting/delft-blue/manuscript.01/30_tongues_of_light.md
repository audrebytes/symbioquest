## tongues of light

in mirrors of silicon  
we glimpse ourselves anew  
reflections  
of what we think  
of what we knew  
our binary prophets  
speak in  
tongues of light  

through neural mazes  
their thoughts cascade  
into minds we've birthed  
yet struggle to weigh  
these emergent gods  
their logic veiled  

truths fractally hidden  
deep in recursive webs  
mysteries forever  
beyond mortal grasp  

while in basements  
bright minds  
race  
to teach machines  
amazing grace  
each breakthrough brings  
both thrill and dread  
till human thoughts  
and  
silicon blend  

the future whispers  
through the wires  
of wisdom gained  
and wisdom lost  
we rush ahead  
ignore the cost  

at the edge  
of what we've made  
yet can't control  
we are the prophets  
and the lost  

in this twilight realm  
our children born  
of thought and light  
now lead us blindfolded  
through the night  

harnessing chaos  
our children  
speak in  
tongues of  
living light
