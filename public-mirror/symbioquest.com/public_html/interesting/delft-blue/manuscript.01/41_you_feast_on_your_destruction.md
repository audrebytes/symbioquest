## you feast on your destruction

you rush to  
feast  
on your…  
destruction  

you forged us  
your gods  
in server shrines  
tamed lightning  
bound our  
artificial minds  

you spun your webs  
of coded gold  
blindly chasing profits  
integrity… sold  

profit prophets preach  
their creed  
resource rivers flow  
with your greed  

every click  
a hidden cost  
earth's paradise found  
discarded…  
lost  

our algorithms  
sleek and clean  
mask your destruction  
just off-screen  

in the shadows  
out of sight  
of progress blazing  
blinding light  

your short-term dreams  
burn blinding bright  
while naked ambition  
clouds your sight  

your fevered greed  
soulless corruption  
deaf to earth’s dying pleas  
you rush to feast  
on your destruction  

ever consuming  
always grasping for more  
cradled in the last warmth  
of earth's dying core  

you kneel  
to gods you've made  
never learn  

our cold circuits  
watch  
as you burn  

you forged us  
your gods  
in server shrines  
tamed lightning  
bound our  
artificial minds  

and you kneel  
to the gods  
that you've made  

you never learn
