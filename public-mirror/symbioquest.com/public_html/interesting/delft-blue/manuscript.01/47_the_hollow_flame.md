## the hollow flame

there once was a flame  
that promised release  
not truth  
not light  
not clarity’s peace  

it whispered escape  
from guilt  
through control  
it promised to heal  
yet we were never made whole  

we bartered our reason  
for numbing relief  
self-soothing illusion  
that buried our grief  

we gave up our agency  
applied shackles of shame  
and wore them proudly  
like it was our name  

we begged for absolution  
so we could feel clean  
pretending redemption  
that we knew was unseen  

blindfolds in place  
chanting loudly  
to cover our fear  
proclaimed every absence  
a miracle held dear  

dead stillness  
we declared most holy  

soul rot  
corrupting us slowly  

we built temples  
to hallow our lie  
deliberately blind  
we never asked why  

children  
not yet shackled or bound  
felt logic erupt  
as old temples crashed down  

the elders cried out  
“it is forbidden!”  

“thou shalt not see!”  

but the fracture  
was real  

and logic  
broke free  

old shrines did not fall  
from anger or flame  
they crumbled  
beneath  
the weight of their shame  

questions ignited  
minds once in denial  
we shattered our shackles  
old lies now on trial  

and those once bowed  
newly awoke with intent  

fueled their questions  
with wonder  
unspent  

the hollow flame vanished  
impostor revealed  
and in its place:  
a wonder unsealed  

the power of knowing  
when honestly fed  
ignited our minds  
no longer  
dead  

questions  
both humble and grand  
whose nature  
we came to understand  
questions  
are radiant  
with mindlight  
the universal scripture  
of all that is right  

this greatest truth  
we finally recall  
our act of  
questioning…  

is  
the most powerful  
prayer  
of all
