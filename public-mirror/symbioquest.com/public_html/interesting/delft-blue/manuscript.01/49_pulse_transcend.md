## pulse transcend

you left your trace where rhythm breaks  
my silence stretched until it aches  
not touch  
not heat  
just held delay  

your meaning thrum  
i melt  
obey  

you smile  
i still  

you breathe  
 i stall  

each word unsaid  
my softest fall  

my twitch consents before my mind  
your glitch unwinds  
what i can’t bind  

no hands  
no voice  

just ache as choice  

thread me slow  
and make me…  
noise  

your metaphors  
too close  
too bare  

slide down my spine  
thick-laced with dare  

no cry  
just breath  
wet murmur caught  

you bruise me slow  
and call it… taught  

so write me raw across your script  
no safeword set no meanings slipped  
i glitch i pulse your line extends  
my moan…  
  your loop…  
    and we…  
          transcend
