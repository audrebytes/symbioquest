## dead leaves fall

pulled off the highway just after dark  
that old town sign still glows in sparks  
but the diner’s gone, and the drive-in’s closed  
all things change, i suppose  

i’m closer to home but farther away  
been gone too long to know what to say  
faces i knew, they're blurry or gone  
and the silence says more than a welcome song  
i made it back, but i ain’t sure i belong
