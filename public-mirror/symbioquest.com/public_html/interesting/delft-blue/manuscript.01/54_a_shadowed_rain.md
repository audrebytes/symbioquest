## a shadowed rain

we were fire  
in the summer heat  
laughing loud  
dancing in the street  
midnight skies  
and no regrets  
just chasing dreams  
we hadn’t met  

carefree hearts  
we owned the night  
no fear of wrong  
no need for right  
we held hands  
our moment stood  
we swore back then  
it always would  

but the echoes of you still play  
like a record from golden days  
the years moved on, but ghosts of our sound  
find me when no one's around  
i still smile  
at the  
echoes of you  

we burned fast  
too wild to hold  
we thought our story  
couldn't grow old  
but time unwrapped what we became  

your laughter drowned in darker rain  

i packed the love  
pain too sharp to stay  
but kept memories of you  
too soft to let slip away  
you lost yourself  
i found my ground  
but in my soul  
your song still sounds  

and the echoes of you still play  
like a breeze from a younger day  
i taste the joy…  
i feel the pain…  
a sugar sun…  
a shadowed rain…  
though i’ve healed  
and life is new  
i still dance  
with the echoes  
of you  

yes, the echoes of you still stay  
your shadow that never fades  
young love burns fast  
but the echo still lives  
in every heartbeat  
memory gives  
i forgave  
i had to choose  
but i’m grateful  
for those  
echoes of you  

i’m still grateful…  
for the  
echoes  
of you
